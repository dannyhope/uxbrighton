<p>At GSK, we want to supercharge our data capability to better understand our patients and accelerate our ability to discover vaccines and medicines. The Onyx Research Data Platform organization represents a major investment by GSK R&amp;D and Digital &amp; Tech, designed to deliver a step-change in our ability to leverage data, knowledge, and prediction to find new medicines.</p><p>We are a full-stack shop consisting of product and portfolio leadership, data engineering, infrastructure and DevOps, data / metadata / knowledge platforms, and AI/ML and analysis platforms, all geared toward:</p><ul><li><p>Building a next-generation, metadata- and automation-driven data experience for GSK’s scientists, engineers, and decision-makers, increasing productivity and reducing time spent on “data mechanics”</p></li><li><p>Providing best-in-class AI/ML and data analysis environments to accelerate our predictive capabilities and attract top-tier talent</p></li><li><p>Aggressively engineering our data at scale, as one unified asset, to unlock the value of our unique collection of data and predictions in real-time.</p></li></ul><p>Onyx Product Management is at the heart of our mission, ensuring that everything from our infrastructure, to platforms, to end-user facing data assets and environments is designed to maximize our impact on R&amp;D. The Product Management team partners with R&amp;D stakeholders and Onyx leadership to develop a strategic roadmap for all customer-facing aspects of Onyx, including data assets, ontology, Knowledge Graph / semantic search, data / computing / analysis platforms, and data-powered applications.&nbsp;</p><p>We are seeking a talented and experienced product designer to join the product management team. As a product designer II, you will lead the product design strategy, design and development of user-centered solutions to increase the overall experience of our data products and services. You will work closely with product team and engineering teams to translate business goals and user needs into design solutions.</p><h2>In this role you will</h2><ul><li><p>Conduct user research, understanding scientific workflows and pain points by working with scientists and product managers to develop user-friendly and solid user experiences&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Lead the UX strategy and design process, responsible for the concept ideation, wireframes, prototypes, design sprints that address the specific needs of key personas;</p></li><li><p>Validate product concept effectively by developing prototypes and interactive mockups with scientific customers and engineers;</p></li><li><p>Collaborate with product managers to define requirements, prioritize features and iterate design solutions;</p></li><li><p>Engage with users effectively, applying strategic thinking and user-centered methodology to communicate the value of key solutions;</p></li></ul><h2>Why you?</h2><h3>Qualifications &amp;
