<p>Jisc is the UK digital, data and technology agency focused on tertiary education, research, and innovation. We are a not-for-profit organisation with a big ambition to improve lives through education and research by providing hardware, software, and networking solutions.</p><p>Join us and your contribution will help us to empower UK universities, colleges, research institutions and wider customers with technology so that everyone prospers.</p><h2>The team</h2><p>The OpenAthens product team manages the OpenAthens portfolio and drives the product vision. The team’s objective is to make the products easier to use, accessible, sustainable and being able to scale with the growing number of customers globally. We exist to remove barriers to knowledge and connect people to information. <br><br>Find out more about Jisc and OpenAthens here: <a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" href="https://www.openathens.net/our-story/">https://www.openathens.net/our-story/</a></p><h2>About the role</h2><p>We are seeking a highly motivated and skilled Lead Researcher to join the team. You will be part of defining the way the UX research is run, working in collaboration with our cross-functional team, made up of UX Designers, Product Managers and Data Analysts. You will be passionate about user experience, possess strong research skills, and have the experience and knowledge to lead our product teams in navigating and understanding a complex landscape of users and technologies.<br><br>You’ll play a pivotal role in understanding our customer needs, behaviours, and preferences, helping to ensure our products are intuitive, engaging, and user centred. You’ll conduct various research activities across our products, analyse data, and provide actionable insights that help drive informed decision-making in our design and development processes.</p><h2>You will be responsible for</h2><p>User Research: Planning, designing, and executing of user research studies using appropriate methodologies, such as interviews, surveys, usability testing, and analytics. Collecting analysing qualitative and quantitative data to gain insights into user behaviours, motivations, and pain points.</p><p>User Persona Development: Creating and maintaining user personas to represent target user groups.<br>Usability Testing: Leading on planning and facilitating usability testing sessions to evaluate product prototypes or existing designs. Identify usability issues, gather feedback, and suggesting improvements to enhance the overall user experience.</p><p>Continuous Improvement: Stay up to date with the latest UX research methodologies, tools, and industry trends. Own, maintain and enhance the user research database and research process, championing a user-centric mindset across the organisation.</p><h2>Key Skills and Experience</h2><ul><li><p>Proven experience in leading and conducting user research and usability testing in an industry setting.</p></li><li><p>Demonstrable knowledge of the latest research methodologies, such as interviews, surveys, and usability testing.</p></li><li><p>Expertise with UX design principles and user-centred design methodologies.</p></li><li><p>Proficiency in qualitative and quantitative data analysis techniques.</p></li><li><p>Excellent communication and presentation skills, with the ability to convey complex research findings to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.</p></li></ul><p>Please refer to the job description for full details. <a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" href="https://jobs.jisc.ac.uk/Jisc/JobDescription/DAnzgwzMDIY#">Lead Researcher- OpenAthens JD.pdf</a></p><p>Don’t meet every single requirement?
