<h2>Join Police Digital Service as Principal UX Designer&nbsp;</h2><p>We are seeking a skilled Principal UX Designer to join the Corporate ICT Team at PDS. As the Principal UX Designer, you will play a crucial role in leading the UX design efforts across a range of digital products and services.</p><h2>Key responsibilities</h2><ul><li><p>Lead the design of user experiences for a range of digital products and services, from initial concept to final implementation.&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Collaborate with product managers, developers, and other stakeholders to define user requirements, ensure design solutions meet business objectives and user needs.&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs that communicate design concepts and interactions.&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Conduct user research, usability testing, and other user-centered design activities to validate design decisions and gather insights to inform design solutions.&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Ensure that designs meet accessibility, usability, and other industry standards and best practices.&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Manage and mentor a team of UX designers, providing guidance and support as needed.&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Develop and maintain design systems, style guides, and design templates to ensure consistency across digital products and services.&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Partner with stakeholders to define and prioritize UX projects and ensure that design solutions are delivered on time and within budget.&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Stay up-to-date with emerging UX design trends, tools, and technologies and evaluate their potential impact on our digital products and services.&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Provide input into product roadmaps and release plans based on user insights and design considerations.&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Foster a culture of innovation, collaboration, and continuous improvement within the UX team and across the organisation.&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Facilitate UX workshops and design sprints to ideate, prioritise, and validate design concepts.&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Work with developers to ensure that designs are implemented accurately and efficiently.&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Identify opportunities for process improvements and optimize workflows to improve efficiency and productivity.&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure that user experience considerations are integrated into all aspects of product development.&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Conduct competitive analysis and benchmarking to inform design decisions and ensure that our digital products and services are competitive in the market.&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Communicate design concepts and solutions effectively to stakeholders and other team members.&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Continuously measure and evaluate the effectiveness of design solutions and iterate as needed based on user feedback and data.&nbsp;</p></li></ul><h2>Key Criteria</h2><ul><li><p>Proven experience of developing and embedding UX services, to mature and
