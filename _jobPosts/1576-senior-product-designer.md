So, it’s not surprising that you’ll find us in every home in the UK, our productions are famous all over the world and we’re at the forefront of the digital streaming revolution.&nbsp;</p><p>When you join us, you enter a fun working environment. With opportunities to learn, to grow and make a real difference. Small enough that your impact’s felt in the business, but big enough that your impact reaches millions of people.&nbsp;</p><p>Come develop your skills, change TV and the course of your career. You will also help other teams occasionally.<br></p><p>Day-to-day you will work in a small team that has a start-up feel and pace to it, guiding the team to think in design best practices. To evolve your product, you will use your T shaped skill set of service design, workshop facilitation and UX/UI skills to collaboratively prioritise problems and ideate solutions with measurable success. You are comfortable going 0 to 1 with little more than high-level guidance. You can block out the noise and deliver effectively.<br><br>You must have proven experience applying UX and UI design skills to large projects. content workflow landscape.</p><h2>Key Responsibilities</h2><ul><li><p>Contribute to the design transformation initiative for CS&amp;D</p></li><li><p>Work in a dynamic and agile manner to mature
