Working in this way, we bring together the best of digital and in-person working in a modern, progressive organisation. Consider their business objectives and align research goals to meet their needs. Use your expertise to support the development of inclusive products, services, and experiences for people with hearing loss.</p></li><li><p>Contribute to the enhancement of user research processes within RNID. Identify opportunities for streamlining and optimising research methodologies, data collection, and analysis techniques. Implement best practices and develop guidelines to ensure research is conducted ethically, efficiently, and effectively.</p></li><li><p>Analyse research data and extract meaningful insights and trends. Synthesize findings into actionable recommendations for improving products, services, and user experiences.</p></li><li><p>Present research findings and insights to internal teams, external stakeholders, and corporate partners in a clear and accessible manner. Select and use appropriate formats for communicating research findings to varied stakeholders, including senior leaders, designers, and people in technical roles.</p></li><li><p>Contribute to the development and management of our user research insights library. Effectively communicate research findings, insights, and recommendations to stakeholders at all levels, ensuring understanding and alignment across the organization. Work collaboratively across RNID to integrate user research findings into evidence-based decision making across the charity.</p></li><li><p>Adhere to ethical guidelines for user research, ensuring participant privacy, informed consent, and data protection throughout the research process.
