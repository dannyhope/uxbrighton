<h2>About the role</h2><p>We are looking for a Senior UX designer driven to build detailed and easy to use products and services for our colleagues &amp; suppliers. Our talented and collaborative multidisciplinary team is made up of design and research specialists, focusing on supporting users to collaborate more efficiently &amp; make better decisions. You will be a part of the product &amp; supplier team, where you will focus on both new and existing product and service experiences. You will champion our colleague &amp; supplier’s needs; discover their underlying challenges and work with technology to build and maintain a high-quality and consistent user experience across your domain. With a genuine appetite for sophisticated information, you will have deep experience in user research, facilitation, conceptualisation, and technical implementation, as well as a passion and energy for design and visual communication of your ideas. Insights and data (quant and qual) are what drive our team, and you will use it to support your decisions. We would love someone passionate about UX, love to understand our people and are excited to improve the experience of colleagues and support their evolving needs.</p><h2>You will be responsible for</h2><p>&nbsp;• Redefining complicated experiences into simple and intuitive solutions</p><p>• Take ownership of all end-to-end UX process activities, and champion human-centred experience design within the immediate project area and in wider business</p><p>• Use appropriate UX research and design methods to deeply understand the user needs, goals, difficulties, and the underlying opportunities to improve our products and services, collaborating with the project team throughout the journey</p><p>• Apply deep systems thinking, service design, and think beyond the digital touchpoints or UI improvements as an efficient solution</p><p>• Coordinate and conduct user-first discoveries, ethnographic research, as well as other appropriate behavioural and attitudinal research to help advise and evaluate design</p><p>• Work with multidisciplinary teams across the business to facilitate business partner (business and user) workshops, run appropriate conceptualisation and participatory design sessions</p><p>• Storyboard, sketch, prototype, and craft fabulous UI solutions that have real impact on the day-to-day lives of our users</p><p>• Work with feature development teams to make sure that the workflow reflects the user needs and ensure consistency among features and similar tools</p><p>• Develop expert-level knowledge of competitive and complementary products and bring new insights to the team</p><p>• Work across the company’s multi-platform sites: and support experiences developed based on adaptable design principles while supporting to the development of our design system</p><p>• Clearly present various stages of design development to business owners, team members and other business partners</p><h2>You will need</h2><p>• Deep expertise in user research, end-to-end design process, conceptualisation, UI prototyping, user testing, and implementation</p><p>• Experience in service design and systems thinking. You can approach design process holistically considering all key touchpoints (digital and non-digital), and are able to think beyond UI design as a go to solution</p><p>• Expertise with a range of quantitative and qualitative research methods, and ability to measure design impact</p><p>• Expertise in human centred discovery and ethnographic research. You’ll have direct and unlimited access to our users</p><p>• Expertise in appropriate research and design methods to deeply understand the as-is, and propose to-be experiences</p><p>• Expertise in human factors, user psychology and what makes people tick 2</p><p>• Proven understanding of front-end, back-end, and the software development life cycle would be a phenomenal advantage (no coding required)</p><p>• Ability to work in partnership with Product Managers, peer UX and UI Designers, and Engineering to align user-centred design vision and activities with our business goals</p><p>• Bachelor’s degree or experience in Interaction Design, Computer Interaction, Behavioural Psychology, or another related field</p><p>• Proficiency with the following tools: Miro, Figma, and other related software</p><p>• Strong UX portfolio highlighting relevant case studies. Displayed experience of user research, deep understanding of existing challenges, and collaboratively producing appropriate solutions are key</p><p>• Flexibility, ability to plan and coordinate, responsiveness, creativity, and a self-starter demeanour</p><p>• Able to build working relationships with peers and leadership</p><p>• Ability to coach, mentor and set standards for ways of working for team members</p><p>• Ability to demonstrate strong written, verbal communication and presentation skills to all levels of seniority and subject areas within the organisation</p><p>• This role will best suit an individual who adopts unique abilities of each team member and seeks team effort over individual achievements.
