<h2>The Team &amp; Role</h2><p>We are looking for a remote Product Designer with a specialized focus on guest and consumer experience who will be responsible for conceptualizing and designing a cohesive experience across our different software solutions. Your work will be rooted in designing features that excite and delight diners as they reserve and book personalized experiences from venues leveraging the SevenRooms platform. You will join a high-performing Design Team and report directly to our Product Design Lead.</p><p>Guest Experience (or GX for short) is a team specifically focused on - you guessed it - the guest experience. This consumer and internal user-facing team builds next-generation booking, ordering, and event reservation experiences. Leveraging all the power and customizability of the SevenRooms platform, this fun and ambitious team is looking for a user-focused, data-driven designer looking to innovate in the most visible aspect of our product, owning an entire user experience from end to end.</p><p>Product Designers at SevenRooms operate in a shared team, working alongside tenured team members with varied skill sets. We’ve built our product team with the most creative, driven, and passionate entrepreneurs and foodies we could find. This small but mighty team is incredibly collaborative while having the independence to find their niche and have a large impact. We are the bridge between our clients and our platform, and we absolutely love what we do (and we love doing it together).</p><h2>What You’ll Do</h2><ul><li><p><strong>User Experience Design: </strong>Conceptualize and design end-to-end user flows that minimize friction and accomplish goals for restaurant operators, allowing user needs and pain points to inform innovative design solutions.</p></li><li><p><strong>UI and Visual Design: </strong>Design beautiful, modern, and functional high-fidelity interfaces that hold their own alongside the best digital consumer products in the market and can be subtly customized to allow customer branding. Own a set of reusable design patterns specifically for SevenRooms' consumer-facing products.</p></li><li><p><strong>UX Research and Usability Testing:</strong> Lead design and product management teams in discovery research to identify users' needs, goals, and pain points; Conduct frequent usability tests on flows for constant iteration.</p></li><li><p><strong>Collaboration: </strong>Collaborate closely with the Product Design Team to review and hone your designs regularly and help others hone theirs.
