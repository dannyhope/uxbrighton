<h2>About the role</h2><p>We're looking for a highly creative and experienced Designer to join a multidisciplinary team working across our editorial, website and app, as part of the Product Design team, as part of your role you will lead projects and mentor other designers.</p><p>Working closely with Lead Designers and the Design Director; you'll be shaping how we tell stories digitally, working on innovative formats to create experiences that are a joy to read. You'll use the newspaper and its supplements as a reference and be unafraid to challenge briefs, pushing the boundaries of what is possible to elevate and convey our journalism to the highest standard. Collaborating with editorial desks and the visual journalism team, providing visual guidance and support to producers, developers, editors and journalists to ensure visual excellence across all we do.</p><p>Your role will be to lead projects that go beyond expectations across the concepts developed for our day-to-day and high profile journalism. You will oversee work for one or more areas of the team's daily, weekly and longer term output.</p><p>You are independent, autonomous and demonstrate an exceptional eye for detail and have experience in dealing with many different stakeholders. The successful candidate will be passionate about design and eager to work with wider teams in realising all design ideas through to delivery.</p><p>Communication is important so you will be comfortable presenting, and able to confidently justify your design decisions. journalists to come up with the best possible solution.</p></li><li><p>You’ll be comfortable working as part of a group, as well as alone, and able to articulate the rationale behind your design decisions to others.</p></li><li><p>You’ll be responsible for mentoring junior designers, sharing insights and providing guidance, feedback and support.</p></li></ul><h2>Key
