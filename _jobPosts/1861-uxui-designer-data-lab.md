We work with our partners to use advances in data and technology to improve lives, protect nature, and ensure just transitions. The Data Lab is composed of WRI’s core Engineering, Product, and Data teams and supports a wide network of quantitative researchers, data scientists and policy analysts embedded across the organization. About some of the products you will work on:&nbsp;TerraMatch, Land and Carbon Lab. Through its online platform, the&nbsp;TerraMatch&nbsp;team identifies the world’s best&nbsp;locally-led&nbsp;tree-growing organizations, screens their project proposals according to industry-leading criteria, and provides them with technical assistance to improve their impact. For projects that successfully raise capital from&nbsp;TerraMatch’s&nbsp;associated funds, the team tracks their progress over time by deploying innovative monitoring, reporting, and verification (MRV) techniques. The Land &amp; Carbon Lab is an innovation platform for geospatial data and monitoring of the world’s land and nature-based carbon.
