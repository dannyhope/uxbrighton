<p><strong>About the Role</strong></p><p>Security Services industry is constantly changing, requiring Citi to react and plan accordingly.&nbsp; An analysis has been undertaken to identify a strategic vision in response to a changing landscape.&nbsp; The business recognises that investment in technology is critical to fulfilling evolving client needs. To that extent, a 5-year transformational strategy is underway focused on platform efficiency, consistent global operating model, capabilities, to support evolving client needs, data strategy and value-added services.</p><p>The Senior UX Designer sits within the Data, Digital and Innovation group under Securities Services Technology. &nbsp;A senior level role, acting as a thought leader in the research, analysis and design of digital products and systems. User Experience partners with Product and Technology colleagues to create innovative and intuitive digital products that deliver best-in-class user experience and usability to our clients, both internally and externally. We undertake a wide spectrum of activities, from discovery and research, through definition, ideation, and design, to the management of design systems and the delivery of interactive prototypes and high-fidelity designs to our development partners.</p><p>The UX Design Group’s primary goal is to research and design products and solutions that are streamlined, intuitive, consistent, accessible, great-looking, and easy-to-use, which perfectly suit (or even anticipate) our clients’ needs.</p><p>This position reports to the ICG Central Team and Head of Data, Digital and Innovation Delivery</p><h2><br>Responsibilities</h2><ul><li><p>Lead a&nbsp;medium-sized&nbsp;design team, managing&nbsp;multiple&nbsp;projects from start to completion, with overall responsibility for delivery.</p></li><li><p>Lead meetings with users, clients, product sponsors and technology to understand project requirements.</p></li><li><p>Plan and perform UX research, define IA, user journeys, and create personas.</p></li><li><p>Identify metrics and data that can support decision-making, guiding tech partners where needed to add necessary metrics.</p></li><li><p>Work closely with users, stakeholders, business analysts, product managers and development teams to brainstorm and co-create ideas, approaches, and solutions.</p></li><li><p>Perform relevant testing to validate and inform design decisions</p></li><li><p>Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs&nbsp;using specialist tools such as Sketch, InVision, Axure, and the Adobe suite.</p></li><li><p>Apply a positive and constructive attitude to all feedback</p></li><li><p>Use and contribute to the approved design system</p></li><li><p>Contribute to design standards and best practices</p></li><li><p>Perform detailed handover to development teams, ensuring they have all they need to implement the designs.</p></li><li><p>Provide ongoing support to development, with regular UX review checkpoints ensuring quality and spec are as designed.</p></li><li><p>Identify and articulate design and technical conflicts between colleagues and/or local stakeholders and help to resolve these.</p></li><li><p>Promote the value of UX within the wider&nbsp;organization.</p></li><li><p>Contribute to the wider UX community of practice.</p></li><li><p>Keep up to date with the latest UX
