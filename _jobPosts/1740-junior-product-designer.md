<h2>Overview</h2><p>This is an exciting opportunity for a junior product designer to work alongside a passionate design team to support the delivery of globally recognised OTT streaming products here at FX Digital.</p><p>Working across the media and sports sectors, you will be responsible for crafting intuitive and beautiful user experiences that shape the future of OTT. You will be part of a growing team and have a passion for professional development with a commitment to teamwork. This is a full-time role based out of our office in Old Street, London.</p><h2>What are your responsibilities?</h2><p>A junior product designer at FX will have exposure to many elements of product and design; this is not a traditional design or product-focused role; this role spans both disciplines in an agency environment working across multiple clients.</p><p>You will be joining the Product Design team here at FX, working closely and being mentored by our mid-weight designer and Creative Director. Your role will include the following key responsibilities:</p><ul><li><p>Capturing product requirements and writing user stories</p></li><li><p>Designing UI elements, screens, and user journeys for Connected TV Streaming products</p></li><li><p>Supporting the Marketing department by creating design assets for internal presentations, social channels, and events.</p></li></ul><h2>Who is this role suitable for?</h2><p>This role is ideal for someone with some design experience but is early in their career or has previously been a graphic designer and is looking to transition into product design.</p><h2>Coaching &amp; Mentoring</h2><p>You will receive mentorship from our mid-weight designer, helping you develop your product design skills. You will also receive coaching from our Co-founder, who leads the Product Design team to support growth in your career.</p><p>Coaching typically involves lots of role-playing, including practice design presentations, client communication, and team dynamics.</p><h2>Working in the office</h2><p>As a junior member of the Product Design team, you will be expected to be in the office 5 days a week. This is to ensure you have continuous face time with mentors and coaches to support your professional growth.</p><p>Please note that many of the roles and departments here at FX have a three-day-in-the-office policy with the option to work remotely for 2 days of the week. In the Product Design department, this becomes available once you are promoted to mid-weight.</p><p>Please do not apply if you are looking for a remote role or a hybrid role with working from home days; this is an in-office product design role.</p><h2>Responsibilities</h2><p>Below is a more detailed breakdown of your key responsibilities:</p><h3>Product Responsibilities</h3><ul><li><p>Supporting discovery workshopping activities, including preparing Miro workshop boards and setting up the room.</p></li><li><p>Support the creation of user stories that detail the expected behaviour of functionality, including user acceptance criteria.</p></li><li><p>Become familiar with and learn about the OTT industry and the key concepts that enable us to build successful streaming products across Smart TVs, streaming sticks, and set-top boxes.</p></li></ul><h3>User Experience Design</h3><p>Your key responsibilities across UX design will include the following:</p><ul><li><p>Supporting research activities across interviews &amp; user testing</p></li><li><p>Creating user journey diagrams</p></li><li><p>Designing low-fidelity wireframes and prototype basic user journeys.</p></li></ul><p>A junior designer will be expected to have a base level of UX skills across the following:</p><ul><li><p>You can recreate an application’s user flow. Your diagrams are easy to follow and edit. They tend to be a high-level view of an application.</p></li><li><p>You can recreate an application's information architecture by reviewing a current application and translating this into a site map. You make intuitive improvements to a product's site map based on research.</p></li><li><p>Building several simple prototypes to test the work you've produced and test them on users before iterating based on feedback.</p></li><li><p>Please note that mentoring will be provided to support you in growth areas.</p></li></ul><h3>User Interface Design</h3><p>Your key responsibilities across UI design will include the following:</p><ul><li><p>Creating and maintaining Design systems in Figma, including brand assets and UI components.</p></li><li><p>Designing key screens for Connected TV streaming applications.</p></li><li><p>Ensuring design projects are organised with clear labels across pages, components, and screens.</p></li><li><p>Designing complete user journeys screen by screen to demonstrate expected behaviour, providing annotations where required</p></li></ul><p>A junior designer will be expected to have a base level of UI skills across the following:</p><ul><li><p>You can follow a style guide to produce smaller features and have a good grounding in typography, colour, and layout.</p></li><li><p>You can adapt and work from a well-defined design system, following the rules and best practices within the system to create components in a digital project. You can take a current design system to adapt &amp; improve it.</p></li><li><p>You know key accessibility principles across digital design and incorporate the fundamentals into your work.</p></li><li><p>Please note that mentoring will be provided to support you in growth areas.</p></li></ul><h3>Creative Artworker (Marketing)</h3><p>You will help support the Marketing team in creating design assets such as the ones listed below:</p><ul><li><p>Design several digital assets to support the marketing department, including newsletter templates, email signatures, and website content.</p></li><li><p>Design professional decks for internal and external presentations</p></li><li><p>Create a range of social media assets, including videos, feature images, carousels, and illustrations to help support and promote the business.</p></li><li><p>Design print assets such as branded merchandise and collateral.</p></li><li><p>Create video content for use across the FX Digital website, social media, and at events. This will include items such as company showreels, video case studies, interviews, creating title screens, lower thirds, and graphical overlays.</p></li><li><p>Support project teams and make our documentation look professional; this would include creating Notion templates &amp; Word doc templates.</p></li><li><p>Design a range of assets to support events where FX Digital are attending, hosting, presenting, or being represented. Mockup visuals for custom event stands and create supporting high-res print-ready visuals such as wall art and flyers.</p></li></ul><h3>Future</h3><p>The Product Design team is a small department, meaning there are many opportunities to take on further responsibilities and grow in your role and career.</p><h2>Why work for FX</h2><ul><li><p>MacBook on your first day at FX</p></li><li><p>Annual profit share is distributed equally across all roles and departments.</p></li><li><p>Pension contributions with Nest pensions</p></li><li><p>Generous 35 days of annual leave, including bank holidays and Christmas office shutdown</p></li><li><p>Monthly one-on-one Sanctus mental health coaching session</p></li><li><p>Optional Vitality Health Insurance with discounted Apple Watch, cinema tickets, and Amazon Prime</p></li><li><p>Cycle-to-work scheme with up to 47% savings on a new bike</p></li><li><p>Flexi start time between 9am and 10am</p></li><li><p>Employee referral scheme</p></li><li><p>Regular appraisals for feedback and growth</p></li><li><p>Weekly dedicated training time for personal development</p></li><li><p>Regular social events</p></li><li><p>Collaborate with prestigious global brands in media, sports, and entertainment.</p></li></ul><p>This is a full-time role based out of our office in Old Street, London. We can only consider applicants who are able to work 5 days per week from the office.</p><p>Thanks for taking the time to learn about this role.
