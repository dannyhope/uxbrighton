<h2>Mission</h2><p>The UX/UI Designer is responsible for solving our clients' ecommerce problems&nbsp; and challenges with smart creative design solutions. They will work closely with Project Managers, Developers, as well as members of the Senior Management team, to achieve a client’s business goals.&nbsp;</p><p>They will apply a visual design approach to everything and will foster a culture of constant optimisation with regards to usability and developing world class user experiences that drive conversions. Identify these user needs and in the form of prototyping, test ideas and refine designs based on data and user feedback.</p></li><li><p>Create and provide attractive designs that are equally usable and aesthetically mature and on brand for our customers.&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Produce and deliver consistent, user focused designs across mobile, tablet and desktop by creating high quality design concepts, wireframes and prototypes that accurately reflect a design language or brand guideline. Produce user flows, wireframes, prototypes, and final design as deliverables for the technology team for all platforms (desktop, mobile, tablet, apps)</p></li><li><p>Lead the conversation and advise customers on the best features and functionality to enhance the user journey and deliver bottom-line growth.</p></li><li><p>Develop a UX community of practice within GPMD and stay up to date on the latest research methods and UX and UI trends, sharing best practices and informing on these trends.</p></li><li><p>Document and lead creation of design standards to be followed by existing and new team members.</p></li><li><p>Stay up to date with the ecommerce platforms and partners we work with and have an awareness of limitations and impacts to overall project costs before presenting design ideas to customers.</p></li><li><p>Work with project managers and frontend engineers to deliver design deliverables such as prototypes, information architecture (IA) diagrams, workflows and wireframes, as well as final designs for implementation.</p></li><li><p>Advise the QA team to ensure that products/features meet requirements across various user journey personas.</p></li><li><p>Solid understanding of the relationship between UX/UI and SEO and accessibility and close collaboration with developers to ensure consistency.</p></li><li><p>Experience in creating design systems that enhances the UI approach across our client's ecommerce websites.
