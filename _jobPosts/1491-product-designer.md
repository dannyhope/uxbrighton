practitioners.</p></li></ul><h3>Managing and developing our people</h3><ul><li><p>Support the development of the design team, including recruitment, coaching, and mentoring of designers.</p></li><li><p>Provide coaching to business stakeholders and practitioners from other Centres of Excellence on Design Thinking.</p></li></ul><h3>Risk and compliance</h3><ul><li><p>Work in
