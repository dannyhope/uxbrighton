Join us on our mission to transform the world, one student, one teacher, and one community at a time.</p><p>The UX Product Design Manager oversees an entire product vertical within our portfolio of products and manages the associated design team. This is not an ivory tower management role; the incumbent should expect to contribute at the individual level as needed. They will be the expert on design decisions made for their product vertical and will knowingly share competitive insights and identify areas of UX and design opportunity. This role connects design initiatives to the product vision and aligns them to the larger organization's goals, strategy, and objectives for their team. They create the visual and functional requirements for the products in their domain, work with Product Manager’s (PM) and engineering to create epics and stories, understand technical limitations and specifications, and ensure product features are built to spec. They govern the design system for their vertical, ensuring that components are built correctly and updated as needed. They also work with the UX Design Director to present strategies, design decisions, and future design vision to executive leadership. The UX Product Design Manager develops processes, sets standards, evaluates progress, and manages designers on their team. They also oversee budgets, vendors, contracts, and hiring decisions within their domain.</p><h2>As a UX Product Design Manager you will...</h2><ul><li><p>Manage a team of UX designers for a product
